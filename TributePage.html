LATA MANGESHKAR

Biography

Lata Mangeshkar was an Indian playback singer and music director. She was one of the best-known and most respected playback singers in India. Mangeshkar's career spanned more than seven decades. She recorded songs in over a thousand Hindi films and has sung songs in over thirty-six regional Indian languages and foreign languages.

She was awarded the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honour, in 2001. She is the second vocalist, after M. S. Subbulakshmi, to have ever been awarded the Bharat Ratna.

Achievements

  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honour, in 2001.
  • Received the Dadasaheb Phalke Award, India's highest award in cinema, in 1989.
  • Received the Padma Bhushan in 1969 and the Padma Vibhushan in 1999.
  • Recorded songs in over a thousand Hindi films and has sung songs in over thirty-six regional Indian languages and foreign languages.
  • Set the world record for the most recorded artist in music history by the Guinness Book of World Records.

Tribute

Let us remember and honor the legendary Lata Mangeshkar for her unparalleled contributions to Indian music. Her soulful voice has left an indelible mark on the hearts of millions, and her legacy will continue to inspire generations to come.
